Resources

  • Counseling Services

    At Tranquil minds, we have a strong group of therapists who are highly trained in holistic trauma healing. Contact us today to speak with any of our qualified therapists. We offer a diverse group of therapists so you can feel more comfortable opening up to someone who looks like you and may have the same personal experience as you. An anonymous system that ensures you only see your therapist when you come and don’t interact with anyone else besides them if that’s what you would like For some, opening up may be hard that’s why we also offer group therapy sessions. It helps to talk with others, and in a group setting, you interact with people going through the same struggles as you and are committed to healing and growing with you.

  • Assistance for seniors

    Seniors in need of assistance during their counseling time here, are not uncommon and we do have certain services for our older patients: - Retirement Planning - Weekly internet training classes - Older Therapists are available upon request if it makes the patient more comfortable

  • Financial Literacy

    We understand that sometimes people can stay in an unsustainable environment due to financial constraints which is why we offer services such as: - Financial Planning - Job Placement with certain partner companies (if eligible) - Tax services and a CPA are available (if eligible) - Host weekly investing seminars
